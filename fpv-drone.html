Welcome!

Max Speed: 70mph

Flight Time: 9 minutes

Video Resolution: 1000TVL

Range: 0.2 miles

Description:

This is my first FPV Drone build, so I wanted to create a durable and cost-effective drone to practice flying. For that reason, the drone is built with a thicker carbon fiber frame and 2-blade propellers for battery efficiency. I plan to use this drone to practice Acro flying and eventually create drone footage. I had a lot of fun building this drone and I hope you find it as intersting as I do.
